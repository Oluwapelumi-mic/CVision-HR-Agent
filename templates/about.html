Transforming Recruitment Through AI-Powered CV Analysis

Created by Iyere Michael (Adedeji), CVision leverages cutting-edge OCR, NLP, and machine learning technologies to automatically analyze resumes against job requirements, helping HR teams identify top talent quickly and objectively.

Our Mission

At CVision, we're committed to transforming the traditional recruitment landscape by eliminating time-consuming manual resume reviews, reducing unconscious bias in candidate selection, and empowering organizations to make data-driven hiring decisions. Our platform is built to help you discover the perfect candidate match efficiently and effectively.

How CVision Works

Document Processing

CVision seamlessly processes CV documents in multiple formats (PDF, Word, plain text) and even scanned images through advanced OCR technology.

Information Extraction

Through sophisticated NER and section classification algorithms, CVision extracts and structures candidate information.

Smart Matching

Our platform matches candidate profiles against job requirements using semantic understanding of skills and experience.

Comprehensive Reports

Get clear, actionable insights through detailed reporting with candidate rankings and skill gap analysis.
